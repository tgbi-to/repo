About TGBI-TO

Welcome to the official website of The Guardians Brotherhood Incorporated -The Original

We, MEN AND WOMEN OF GOODWILL AND INTENTION, in good standing in our communities, with the help of God, have come together to promote and maintain our social, intellectual, moral, economic, and general well-being; protect and defend our individual and collective rights; strengthen our fraternal bonds; and enhancing the spirit of unity, brotherhood, justice, righteousness, and self-sufficiency.

From a vision born in the jungles of Mindanao, the Diablo Squad emerge—a Brotherhood forged by courage and Unity, as times past it evolves into Diablo Squad Crime Buster, embracing a deeper commitment to public service, peace and justice, in December 10,1984 this dream took form and became a legal and lasting legacy, The Guardians Brotherhood Incorporated.

For over four decades our organization has weathered countless challenges from internal struggles to external misunderstanding. We have face trials that tested our loyalty our values and even our very identity, but through it all, we never give up, we remained strong, and today we proudly declare to the Philippines and the world, we are stronger than ever!

I am Master Founder Alamid, one of the new breed of leaders who haw taken up the mantle with pride courage and Honor, I carried with me the lessons if the past, and the vision of better tomorrow. It is time to break the chains that have hinted and hindered our Brotherhood for too long, we will not allow, division, conflicts of personal inte6to destroy what generation of Brothers and sisters have built, sweat and loyalty.

Under the firm and guiding leadership of our International Chairman, UPMF CARLOMAGNO one of the original Incorporators of The Guardians Brotherhood Incorporated, we are writing a new chapter, one that is rooted in Unity, purpose and reform, together we aim to bring back the true essence of Brotherhood and reaffirmed our unshakeable commitment to our Founding Principles.

We live by seven Guiding Principles that define our Identity and mission. Brotherhood, Integrity, Peace, Discipline, Service, Equality, Justice. They are not just words, they are the foundation of our organization, the values that bind us together and the commitment we make to every member and to the community we serve.

We invite you to explore the website, to reconnect, to learn and to be inspired by the legacy and future of TGBI-TO, whether you are a founding member a returning Brother or a newly inducted recruit, know that you are part of something bigger than yourself, A family, A movement, A Brotherhood with a mission.

Mission & Vision

OUR MISSION

The Guardians Brotherhood is a fraternity of men and women committed to the tenets of self-development, brotherhood, and charity for the betterment of society and its people of various colors, persuasion, and creed.

About Education

We always keep learning and increasing the amount of knowledge and skills. We readily share what we know openly. We love and respect the creativity of other people. We travel, learn new things and develop ourselves.

About Family

We always treat our family and members of other families with respect. We try to support others in difficult times, rejoice at success, and help with everything we can. We are ready to sacrifice a lot for the family.

About Health

Take care of your appearance, and observe the rules of personal hygiene. Eat only healthy food, drink plenty of clean water, go in for sports, no alcohol, tobacco, or other toxic substances.

About Behavior

Always act according to your conscience. Never overstep the will of another person. Be friendly, courageous, and decent. Respect and help your elders. Do not be offended by the younger and weaker.

About Friends

Friends are a small family. Your environment is a part of you, so choose your friends very carefully. Choose those who are close in spirit, those with whom you develop and grow - kind, strong, honest and decent.

About Speech

Develop eloquence, do not flap. Silence is gold. Tell only the truth. No swear words. Be flexible, be able to find a common language with others. Speak only good things about the absence.

About Loyalty & Responsibility

Remain devoted no matter what. First of all - yourself. Do not be afraid of responsibility, and if you take it, remain responsible to the end. Respect your country and your city.

About Material Well-being

The material component of life is very important. Enrich yourself and your family. Save money. Earn only honestly. Think about the welfare of your children. Take care of their education and future.

Code of Ethics

As an officer/member of THE GUARDIANS BROTHERHOOD, INC-THE ORIGINAL, my fundamental duty is to serve the Association wholeheartedly; adhere to its seven (7) guiding principles of brotherhood, discipline, equality, service, peace, justice, and integrity; treat my fellow officers/members equally with the utmost respect the way I respect myself and want others to respect me; respect the women, the elders, and persons in authority, respect the Constitutional rights of everyone; help my fellow officers/members in times of need to the best of my capability; perform the job inherent to my position diligently, honestly and in good faith; and continuously uphold the honor and integrity of the Association.

I will do my best to keep my private life unsullied as an example to all. Honest in thought and deed, I will be exemplary in obeying the laws of the land and the By-Laws of THE GUARDIANS BROTHERHOOD, INC-THE ORIGINAL. I will harbor no grudges or hatred against anyone, as love, peace, humility, and understanding will reign in my heart. I will not take advantage of my position to promote my self-interest.

I recognize the authority of my leaders and seek their guidance if necessary. In case of any misunderstanding within the Association, I will refrain from airing the same to the public. Still, I will course my concern through the proper procedure, such as the grievance machinery. I will not do any illegal or untoward activity that will put the Association in a bad light. I will take responsibility for my action without passing the blame to others or trying to evade personal liability.

I will constantly strive to achieve these objectives and ideals, dedicating myself before God and my chosen Association, THE GUARDIANS BROTHERHOOD, INC.-THE ORIGINAL.

Our History & Origins

1976

Diablo Squad Formation

The movement begins in the jungles of Mindanao as the military unit Diablo Squad, founded by the legendary Leborio Jangao Jr., known as "BFG ABRAHAM" — the Brain and Father of all GUARDIANS.

1984

Official SEC Registration

On December 10, 1984, The Guardians Brotherhood, Inc. is officially registered with the SEC as a peaceful civil-military legal organization, following the transformation from DSCB (Diablo Squad Crime Buster).

Today

Reform & Unity

Under the leadership of International Chairman UPMF CARLOMAGNO, the organization is implementing reforms to return to its roots, eradicate internal disputes, and unite true brothers and sisters worldwide.

How to Join TGBI-TO

Eligibility Requirements

  • Filipino citizen (foreign nationals with significant contributions may be considered)
  • At least 21 years old (for Magic Group)
  • Good standing in the community
  • Willing to uphold the 7 Guiding Principles
  • Must have a bona fide member as Sponsor

Six-Stage Process

  1. Application: Complete official form endorsed by a Sponsor
  2. Presentation: Present to local Membership Committee
  3. Background Investigation (BI): Verification of credentials
  4. Orientation Seminar: Introduction to organization
  5. Mandatory Basic Course (MBC): 12-hour training program
  6. Indoctrination Rites: Final acceptance into the Brotherhood
