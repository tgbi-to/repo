Our Foundation

Mission & Vision

Our Mission
Our Mission

The Guardians Brotherhood is a fraternity of men and women committed to the tenets of self-development, brotherhood, and charity for the betterment of society and its people of various colors, persuasion, and creed.

Our Vision
Our Vision

1. About Education: We always keep learning and increasing the amount of knowledge and skills. We readily share what we know openly. We love and respect the creativity of other people. We travel, learn new things and develop ourselves.

2. About Family: We always treat our family and members of other families with respect. We try to support others in difficult times, rejoice at success, and help with everything we can. We are ready to sacrifice a lot for the family. We protect their relatives and friends from anything. We build relationships - only for love. We accept others as they are.

3. About health: Take care of your appearance, and observe the rules of personal hygiene. Keep your body clean and tidy. Wash your hands before meals, take a shower regularly, and monitor your body condition. If you have any symptoms, do not hesitate to treat them and think about why this disease appeared. Eat only healthy food, and drink plenty of clean water. Go in for sports, improve your body, and don't run it. No alcohol, tobacco, or other toxic substances. Walk a lot in nature and move, movement is life. Getting enough sleep, good sleep is very important. Observe the regime. Get up earlier. Listen to your body, it can tell you a lot.

4. About behavior: Always act according to your conscience. Never overstep the will of another person. Respect others, but don't be offended by yourself and others. Be friendly, courageous, and decent. Respect and help your elders. Do not be offended by the younger and weaker than you. Do not get mad. Defend your rights and your property. Don't harm another. All people are different - remember that. Help out in trouble. Give to the one asking. Be honest with others as well as with yourself. Take responsibility for your actions and your life. Take responsibility and go to the end. If you chose the path, follow them, make adjustments, and set goals for yourself. Be reasonable and patient. Do not harm nature, do not litter, and keep your home in order and clean. Smile more.

5. About friends: Friends are a small family. Your environment is a part of you, so choose your friends very carefully. Understand people. Choose those who are close in spirit, those with whom you do not stand still, but you develop and grow - kind, strong, honest and decent. Help in trouble.

6. About speech: Develop eloquence, do not flap. Silence is gold. Tell only the truth, and hide it only in the most exceptional moments, for the good of your loved ones and yourself. No, swear words. Be flexible, and be able to find a common language with others. Speak only good things about the absence. Know how to agree in any situation. Do not argue if you are guilty, admit your mistake and apologize. Do not mock others. Don't speak without thinking.

7. About loyalty and responsibility: Remain devoted no matter what. First of all - yourself. Do not be afraid of responsibility, and if you take it, remain responsible to the end. Know how to be responsible for your actions. Make decisions, and be strong. Respect your country, and your city, and remember that this is the environment where you and your children live. Do something useful to develop them.

8. About material well-being: The material component of life is very important. Enrich yourself and your family. Save money. Do not waste your time on trifles. Always find the best choice. Earn only honestly. The money will come and go. Remember that money is not the main thing, but the well-being, life, rest, education, and development of your family depend on it. Don't cheat. Start small and grow. Think about the welfare of your children. Take care of their education and future in advance. Give them a start but don't put them on your neck. Teach and let them go.

Creed of the Guardians Brotherhood

I am GUARDIANS I stand firm in the spirit of Brotherhood. Bound not by blood, but by loyalty, respect, and unity. I live with unwavering Integrity. Doing what is right Even when no one is watching. I uphold Peace Resolving conflicts with reason, Compassion and calm strength. I fight for Justice, defending the innocent, Confronting the anguist, and standing as a shield For those who cannot protect themselves. I believe in Equality, Treating every person with fairness, respect, and dignity, Regardless of race, status or belief. I embrace Discipline In thoughts, in words, and in actions A reflection of Honor and self-mastery. I commit to Service, Placing the welfare of others along with myself, And answering the call of duty without mental reservation With these seven principles as my guide, I walk the path of true Guardians, Faithful to my Brotherhood, Loyal to my Mission and Steadfast in my Purpose. I am GUARDIANS, I endure with Honor, and I live by the CODE.

What We Stand For

Seven Guiding Principles

These principles are the living foundation of every GUARDIANS member. They are not rules imposed from outside — they are values chosen from within.

Principle I

Brotherhood

The cornerstone of our organization. To build strong bonds of camaraderie, oneness, and fellowship among all members — bound by loyalty and mutual respect.

Principle II

Discipline

Self-restraint, calm demeanor, and adherence to the By-Laws. A member must know how to govern themselves before governing others.

Principle III

Equality

Equal application of law for all — protecting the vulnerable and ensuring every member receives fair and impartial treatment under the organization's rules.

Principle IV

Service

Selfless service to oneself, to the organization, and to fellow members — within the limits of one's means and always in good faith.

Principle V

Peace

Each member acts as an ambassador of peace. We advocate friendliness, harmony, and understanding in all our chapters worldwide.

Principle VI

Justice

Justice served according to righteousness, equity, and moral significance. Firm, but always tempered with compassion and due process.

Principle VII

Integrity

Honesty, reliability, and truthfulness in character. Every member's record should be worthy of emulation — unsullied in deed and word.

Our Roots

A Legacy Since 1976

The story of TGBI-TO is one of honor, transformation, and unwavering commitment to the original vision. These are the defining milestones.

1976

The Diablo Squad

BFG Abraham (Leborio Jangao Jr.) organizes the 11-member Diablo Squad in Kidama, Matanog, Maguindanao — the earliest seed of what would become the Brotherhood.

1980

Diablo Squad Crime Buster

The DSCB is established in Iligan City, Lanao del Norte with 15 founding members, including UPMF Carlomagno (member #13), uniting professionals from military and civilian life.

1984

SEC Registration – The Original

Following the disbandment of the DSCB with the blessing of then-Lt. Gen. Fidel V. Ramos, The Guardians Brotherhood, Inc. is formally registered with the SEC on December 10, 1984 under Registration No. 123899.

2012

Universal Guardians Brotherhood

BFG Abraham establishes the UGB, further consolidating the original Diablo Squad '76 pioneers. TGBI-TO supports unification of all GUARDIANS while operating independently under UPMF Carlomagno.

2018

Amended By-Laws

The TGBI-TO By-Laws are amended at the convention in San Nicolas, Pangasinan, formalizing the rank of UPMF and updating the governance structure for the modern era.

Today

Global Expansion

With chapters across Luzon, Visayas, Mindanao, and international regions worldwide, TGBI-TO remains the one true Original — guarding against imitation and continuing its mission of brotherhood and nation-building.

Organization

How We Are Structured

The Brotherhood operates through a clear, accountable hierarchy — from the Guardians Supreme Council down to local Barangay chapters, and across the globe through our International Fraternal Commission.

Guardians Supreme Council

The highest governing body, headed by the International Chairman (UPMF Carlomagno). Oversees all national and international operations through Cabinet Secretaries and Fraternal Commissioners.

International Fraternal Commission

Supervises all chapters outside the Philippines. Each country is considered one Region, led by a Regional Founder who reports to the International Fraternal Commissioner.

Regional & Local Chapters

Active across the Philippines in every region, province, municipality, and barangay. Each chapter is granted autonomy in its administration while bound by the TGBI By-Laws.

The Magic Group (MG)

Civilian professionals and community leaders who form the TGBI's civic backbone. MAGIC: Magistrate Advocates of Grievances of the Impotent Citizen — defenders of those without a voice.

Disciplinary & Grievance System

All disciplinary actions and final dispositions are issued solely by the International Chairman, ensuring due process, fairness, and the protection of every member's rights.

Digital Chapter

The emerging digital wing supporting coordination, communication, and outreach across the Brotherhood's global membership. Powered by members committed to the seven guiding principles.
