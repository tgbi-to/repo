Our Mission & Vision

Our Mission

The Guardians Brotherhood is a fraternity of men and women committed to the tenets of self-development, brotherhood, and charity for the betterment of society and its people of various colors, persuasion, and creed.

We, MEN AND WOMEN OF GOODWILL AND INTENTION, in good standing in our communities, with the help of God, have come together to promote and maintain our social, intellectual, moral, economic, and general well-being; protect and defend our individual and collective rights; strengthen our fraternal bonds; and enhancing the spirit of unity, brotherhood, justice, righteousness, and self-sufficiency.

From a vision born in the jungles of Mindanao, the Diablo Squad emerge—a Brotherhood forged by courage and Unity, as times past it evolves into Diablo Squad Crime buster, embracing a deeper commitment to public service, peace and justice, in December 10,1984 this dream took form and became a legal and lasting legacy, The Guardians Brotherhood, Inc.

For over four decades our organization has weathered countless challenges from internal struggles to external misunderstanding. We have face trials that tested our loyalty our values and even our very identity, but through it all, we never give up, we remained strong, and today we proudly declare to the Philippines and the world, we are stronger than ever!

I am Master Founder Alamid, one of the new breed of leaders who have taken up the mantle with pride courage and Honor, I carried with me the lessons of the past, and the vision of better tomorrow. It is time to break the chains that have hinted and hindered our Brotherhood for too long, we will not allow division, conflicts of personal interest to destroy what generation of Brothers and sisters have built, sweat and loyalty.

Under the firm and guiding leadership of our International Chairman, UPMF CARLOMAGNO one of the original Incorporators of The Guardians Brotherhood Incorporated, we are writing a new chapter, one that is rooted in Unity, purpose and reform, together we aim to bring back the true essence of Brotherhood and reaffirmed our unshakeable commitment to our Founding Principles.

We live by seven Guiding Principles that define our Identity and mission: Brotherhood, Integrity, Peace, Discipline, Service, Equality, Justice. They are not just words, they are the foundation of our organization, the values that bind us together and the commitment we make to every member and to the community we serve.

We invite you to explore the website, to reconnect, to learn and to be inspired by the legacy and future of TGBI-TO, whether you are a founding member a returning Brother or a newly inducted recruit, know that you are part of something bigger than yourself — A family, A movement, A Brotherhood with a mission.

Core Values

Seven Guiding Principles

Principle I

Brotherhood

The cornerstone of our organization. To build strong bonds of camaraderie, oneness, and fellowship among all members — bound by loyalty and mutual respect.

Principle II

Integrity

Honesty, reliability, and truthfulness in character. Every member's record should be worthy of emulation — unsullied in deed and word.

Principle III

Peace

Each member acts as an ambassador of peace. We advocate friendliness, harmony, and understanding in all our chapters worldwide.

Principle IV

Justice

Justice served according to righteousness, equity, and moral significance. Firm, but always tempered with compassion and due process.

Principle V

Equality

Equal application of law for all — protecting the vulnerable and ensuring every member receives fair and impartial treatment under the organization's rules.

Principle VI

Discipline

Self-restraint, calm demeanor, and adherence to the By-Laws. A member must know how to govern themselves before governing others.

Principle VII

Service

Selfless service to oneself, to the organization, and to fellow members — within the limits of one's means and always in good faith.

Our Code

Code of Ethics

As an officer/member of THE GUARDIANS BROTHERHOOD, INC-THE ORIGINAL, my fundamental duty is to serve the Association wholeheartedly; adhere to its seven guiding principles; treat my fellow officers/members equally with the utmost respect the way I respect myself and want others to respect me; respect the women, the elders, and persons in authority; respect the Constitutional rights of everyone; help my fellow officers/members in times of need to the best of my capability; perform the job inherent to my position diligently, honestly and in good faith; and continuously uphold the honor and integrity of the Association.

I will do my best to keep my private life unsullied as an example to all. Honest in thought and deed, I will be exemplary in obeying the laws of the land and the By-Laws of THE GUARDIANS BROTHERHOOD, INC-THE ORIGINAL. I will harbor no grudges or hatred against anyone, as love, peace, humility, and understanding will reign in my heart. I will not take advantage of my position to promote my self-interest.

I recognize the authority of my leaders and seek their guidance if necessary. In case of any misunderstanding within the Association, I will refrain from airing the same to the public, but I will course my concern through the proper procedure, such as the grievance machinery. I will not do any illegal or untoward activity that will put the Association in a bad light. I will take responsibility for my action without passing the blame to others or trying to evade personal liability.

I will constantly strive to achieve these objectives and ideals, dedicating myself before God and my chosen Association, THE GUARDIANS BROTHERHOOD, INC-THE ORIGINAL.

Our Legacy

History of TGBI-TO

1976

The Diablo Squad is Born

BFG Abraham (Leborio Jangao Jr.) organizes the 11-member Diablo Squad in Kidama, Matanog, Maguindanao — the earliest seed of what would become the Brotherhood.

1980

Diablo Squad Crime Buster (DSCB)

The DSCB is established in Iligan City, Lanao del Norte with 15 founding members, including UPMF Carlomagno (member #13), uniting professionals from military and civilian life.

1982

First Convention

The DSCB Constitution and By-Laws are drafted and ratified at Mindanao State University-Iligan Institute of Technology, with the Magic Group (lawyers, professors, leaders) contributing to the early foundations.

1984

SEC Registration – THE ORIGINAL

Following the disbandment of the DSCB with the blessing of then-Lt. Gen. Fidel V. Ramos, The Guardians Brotherhood, Inc. is formally registered with the SEC on December 10, 1984 under Registration No. 123899.

Today

Global Brotherhood

With chapters across Luzon, Visayas, Mindanao, and international regions worldwide, TGBI-TO remains the one true Original — continuing its mission of brotherhood, service, and nation-building under UPMF Carlomagno.
