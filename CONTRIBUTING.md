# Contributing to TGBI-TO Repository

Welcome! This repository is for the official use of The Guardians Brotherhood, Inc. - The Original (TGBI-TO).

## Guidelines for Contributions

- All content must be in English.
- Follow the organizational structure and guidelines as outlined in the Constitution and By-Laws.
- For any changes, please coordinate with the International Council or your local chapter leadership.
- Ensure all contributions align with the 7 Guiding Principles: Brotherhood, Integrity, Peace, Discipline, Service, Equality, and Justice.

## How to Contribute

1. Contact your local chapter leadership or the International Council for approval.
2. Follow the existing file structure and coding conventions.
3. Submit changes through proper channels as determined by the organization.

Thank you for your commitment to the Brotherhood!
